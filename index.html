FAQ

What does G1Flare do?
It converts USDT (USDT0 on Flare) into sFLR — staked Flare — in a single atomic transaction. Your USDT swaps to WFLR on SparkDEX and is staked via Sceptre, with sFLR delivered straight to your wallet.
Is it custodial? Do you hold my funds?
No. The contract holds nothing between transactions. Each swap settles fully or reverts fully — your funds are never parked or left at rest in the contract.
Why sFLR instead of FLR?
sFLR earns Sceptre staking rewards from the moment it lands, and is collateral-grade for Firelight (Phase 2, July 2026). G1Flare mints it at the staking rate, so you skip the slippage of buying sFLR from a liquidity pool.
What's the fee?
0.05% flat, taken in USDT before the swap. No hidden spread.
Can I get plain FLR instead?
G1Flare delivers sFLR, which is liquid immediately — you can hold, sell, or LP it right away while it earns staking rewards. If you specifically want unstaked FLR, you can convert sFLR→FLR via Sceptre (about a 14.5-day unstaking cooldown), or buy FLR directly on SparkDEX.
How do I know it's real?
Every contract is verified live on Flarescan — the FLARESCAN link above goes straight to it. The proof is on-chain, not on this page.
